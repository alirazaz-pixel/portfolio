02 — Why this combination works

A finance brain that codes.

Most people who build Excel dashboards have never sat across from a client explaining capital gains tax. Most people who understand CGT have never built a dashboard. I've spent eleven years on the finance side — and I taught myself to build the tools that finance teams actually need.

That means the models I build don't just look clean. They calculate things correctly: the redemption logic, the tax treatment, the rebalancing math — because I've done it with real money, not just sample data.

Outside Excel, I extend the same thinking into lightweight web apps — personal finance trackers, fleet and asset management systems — built with JavaScript and Firebase, designed for how people actually use their phones in Pakistan.
